WORD BATTLE

Game Rules

  • The game has 3 difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard.
  • Each level has 6 rounds.
  • The first 3 rounds: You describe random words. The AI tries to guess.
  • The next 3 rounds: The AI describes a word. You try to guess.
  • You and the AI each start with 100 seconds.
  • Time runs only on the active player's turn.
  • Wrong answers: -5 seconds
  • Hint usage: -2 seconds
  • Change the word : -2 seconds
  • If your time runs out, the game ends immediately.
  • The winner is the one with more time left at the end!
